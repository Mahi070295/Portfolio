About Me

Background & Skills

Civil engineer turned data analyst, with a strong foundation in structured data and a drive to solve real business problems.

That experience gave me a sharp eye for data quality and a practical understanding of how businesses use data — before I ever wrote a line of SQL.

I hold a Diploma in Data Analytics covering Excel, Power BI, Tableau, SQL, and Python, and a B.Tech in Civil Engineering from ACE College of Engineering, Kerala.
